Simple Tools for architectural  Massing in Google Earth

Step 1: Using Google Earth navigate to the proper latittude and longitude for your property. 

Step 2: Using the polygon tool, draw an shape by clicking the outer boundary of your property line. 

Step 3: Right click polygon in menu on the lefthand side, and SAVE AS a .KML file

Step 4: Open https://michaelvergalla.com/massing-pro/

Step 5: Upload your saved .KML to the massing tool. 

Step 6: Ideate your dream home, with blocks or preset home styles. 

Step 7: Export .kml from the massing tool. 

Step 8: Open downloaded .kml in Google Earth
