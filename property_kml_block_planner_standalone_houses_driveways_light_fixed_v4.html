Property KML Block Planner Pro

Parcel + house massing + per-edge setbacks + driveway paths for Google Earth

Load a parcel KML or a previous massing-study KML to begin.
Import / Export
The importer can reopen prior exported studies by reading block metadata from ExtendedData.
Site Rules
Per-Edge Setbacks
Each parcel edge can have its own setback. The dashed polygon shows the approximate buildable area.
Create
Driveway mode: click the canvas to add points, press Enter to finish, or Escape to cancel drawing.
House Presets
The style preset sets defaults for floors, roof type, roof height, colors, and typical proportions. You can still edit everything after applying it.
Houses
Driveways
Selected House
Orange corner handle rotates. Drag the body to move. The house is checked against the property boundary and the current per-edge setbacks.
Selected Driveway
Click and drag the round driveway handles to reshape the path. Export writes the driveway as segment polygons plus a centerline folder.
Notes

Google Earth works well with simple KML geometry. This tool exports editable house solids and driveway surfaces, grouped into folders with metadata, colors, and opacity preserved.

Steps: parcel import, iterative block re-open, style presets, driveway layout, and shareable KML/KMZ output.
